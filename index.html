Welcome to the PACMAN documentation hub.

PACMAN is a portable C++ library with Python/Fortran bindings for mesh and point-cloud data transfer, featuring coupling, mapping, and adaptive interpolation on CPUs and GPUs through Kokkos & ArborX.
